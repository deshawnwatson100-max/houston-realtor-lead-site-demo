Practice walkthrough script

Set the frame: “I don’t want to just tell you what I build. I want you to experience it like one of your customers would.”

Website: “This is the front door. It gives the customer a clean place to call, text, or submit job details.”

Intake: “Instead of a basic form, this collects quote-ready details so you can respond faster and ask fewer follow-up questions.”

Chat: “If they have a quick question, the website chat keeps them engaged instead of letting them leave.”

Voice AI: “If they call after hours or while you’re busy, the AI receptionist can answer, qualify, and collect what your team needs.”

Backend: “Everything lands in HighLevel so the business owner can manage leads from the app.”
