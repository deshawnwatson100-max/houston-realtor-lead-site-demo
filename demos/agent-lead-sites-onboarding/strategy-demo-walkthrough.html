Call script

Open: “The reason I wanted to show this live is because this is more than a website. It is a system that helps you capture, organize, and respond to junk-removal leads faster.”

Website: “Here is what the customer sees. Instead of just a basic contact form, they can explain the job, choose the type of removal, upload photos, and send details that help you quote faster.”

App: “Now here is what you see as the owner. The request lands in your mobile app as an opportunity. You can open the customer, review the notes, see photo links, and know exactly what they need before you reply.”

Voice AI: “If someone calls instead of filling out the website, the AI receptionist can answer, collect the same job details, and help make sure fewer calls slip through.”

Close: “So what we set up for you is the website, the app workflow, the intake forms, and the AI receptionist. You get a system that helps turn traffic and missed calls into organized quote opportunities.”

Offer close: Lead Website — $500 setup + $100/month. Website + Lead System — $750 setup + $200/month. AI Receptionist — $1,500 setup + $500/month for home services, or $2,500 setup + $750/month for chiropractors/practices. Full Customer-Acquisition System — $3,500 setup + $1,000/month.
