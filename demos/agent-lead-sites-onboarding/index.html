8. Terms of Service

Agent Lead Sites Terms of Service

Project Start: Development begins after onboarding information and payment are received.

Payment: The standard website build is $750 total: a $250 deposit to begin, then a $500 final payment after the website is completed. The $200/month ongoing service begins when the deposit is paid, with the first monthly charge scheduled one month after the deposit date unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Timeline: First draft is typically delivered within approximately 48 hours after complete onboarding and payment. Timelines may shift if required materials are missing.

Client Materials: Client is responsible for providing accurate business information, images, logos, reviews, permissions, and service details.

Revisions: Reasonable revisions are included during the draft/review process. Major scope changes may require additional fees.

Monthly Service: Hosting, maintenance, CRM, support, AI features when applicable, and website updates are provided as part of the active monthly service arrangement.

Ownership & Access: Client content belongs to the client. Agent Lead Sites may manage hosting, CRM, and technical access while service is active.

No Guarantee: Agent Lead Sites builds conversion-focused websites and systems but does not guarantee a specific number of leads, sales, rankings, or revenue.
